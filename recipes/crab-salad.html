Crab salad

Ingredients:

so what about Ingredients u should know that it's depends only oo your taste

  • mayonnaise
  • celery and red onion
  • fresh dill
  • pepper
  • salt
  • old Bay seasoning
  • imitation of crab (taste like fish)

how to cook

  1. Chop or flake the Imitation Crab
  2. add it to a big bowl
  3. add mayonnaise, celery, ref onion
  4. then add dill and Old bay seasoning
  5. sit everything together
  6. season with salt and pepper
  7. have a good meal
