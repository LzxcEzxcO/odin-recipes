Creamy Tomato Pasta

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces uncooked pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2-3 cloves garlic minced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 (14 fluid ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 3/4 cup heavy/whipping cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon Italian seasoning

how to cook

  1. Boil a large, salted pot of water
  2. put in there pasta and cook it ak dente
  3. add butter
  4. add garlic and saute
  5. add tomato sauce, cream, and Italian seasoning
  6. Stir until it's nice and smooth
  7. cook it 5 minutes
  8. Drain the pasta and toss with the sauce
  9. Serve immediately with any add you want
